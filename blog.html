Podcasting, For Us By Us

A podcast is a free audio story, you can listen to anywhere and any time by downloading or streaming it on various platforms. The term itself was coined by technologist Ben Hammersley, as a portmanteau of “iPod” and broadcast. Over the years, we have seen the proliferation of this audio and increasingly visual content on the internet. Podcasting has always had a political spirit, motivated by a belief in the internets ability to democratise speech and circumvent gatekeepers. There are different types of podcasts, comedy, sex & relationships, sports, political commentary, business and sometimes they just tell a story - fact or fiction. In January 2013, comedy and pop culture podcast ‘The Read’, widely held as the pioneer of black podcasting, premiered.
